Shipping policy

At Dreak Trading, we are dedicated to providing you with a seamless shopping experience. To ensure that your order reaches you in a timely and secure manner, please take a moment to review our shipping details:

1. Shipping Policy:
  • We are pleased to offer free shipping within India to make your shopping experience even more convenient and cost-effective.
  • Shipping is insured by our trusted shipping agency to safeguard your purchase during transit.
  • Shipping times may vary depending on the shipping agency, and we cannot be held responsible for shipping delays or customs-related hold-ups.
  • Please note that any refusal to pay customs duties or the rejection of a shipment within three days of its arrival at the destination may result in no refund being provided. In such cases, the shipment may be either destroyed or returned to the shipper's origin, with product price adjustments made accordingly.
2. Delivery Time:
  • For In-Stock products, we aim to deliver within 10 business days.
  • Pre-Order products have a production lead time of 30 business days, with delivery time not included.
3. Freight Policy and Damage Claims:
  • We strongly recommend inspecting your purchase immediately upon delivery.
  • Before signing for any items, ensure that you inspect them for any damage.
  • By signing the receipt, you acknowledge full acceptance of all packages listed on the bill of lading or electronic clipboard in undamaged condition.
